Myles' Recipe for Homemade Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

For the Seasonings and Meat:

  • 1 kg. chicken (preferably thighs)
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 tbsp. brown sugar
  • 1 whole piece onion (medium size)
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp. peppercorn
  • 4 pieces bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp. cooking oil
  • 1 cup of water

For Utensils:

  • Wok
  • Ladel (wood or metal)
  • Spoon

Instructions:

  1. Saute onions and garlic on medium fire until aromatic.
  2. Add chicken.
  3. Saute for a while.
  4. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, peppercorn, and bay leaves.
  5. Add 1 cup of water.
  6. Cover and let it simmer until it is tender (10 minutes).
  7. Season with brown sugar. (Adjust according to taste)
  8. Simmer again until cooked.
  9. Taste the dish to see if there is anything to adjust.
  10. Serve.
